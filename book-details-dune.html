Dune

By Frank Herbert

Availability: Available Now

Summary:

Set in the distant future amidst a sprawling feudal interstellar empire where aristocratic houses control planetary fiefs, Dune tells the story of young Paul Atreides, whose family accepts control of the desert planet Arrakis. As the only source of the most valuable substance in the universe, 'the spice,' Arrakis is a dangerous planet, infested with giant sandworms, and is vital to space travel. Paul is soon embroiled in a plot that aims to destroy his family, leading him to a destiny far greater than he could have imagined.

Genre: Science Fiction, Epic, Adventure
