Author Spotlight: Jane Doe

Join us for an exclusive Q&A session and book signing with best-selling author Jane Doe as she discusses her latest novel, "Whispers in the Stacks."

Jane Doe, the acclaimed author of "The Unread Page" and "Ink & Shadows," will be joining us for an intimate conversation about her writing process, her inspirations, and the intricate world-building in her newest psychological thriller, "Whispers in the Stacks." Attendees will have the opportunity to ask questions during the Q&A segment, followed by a book signing session. Copies of her books will be available for purchase at the event.

Event Details:

  • Date: **Saturday, July 20, 2025**
  • Time: **3:00 PM - 4:30 PM** (Singapore Time)
  • Location: **Main Auditorium, Silent Library**
    123 Library Lane, Knowledge Hub, Singapore 123456
  • Admission: **Free** (Registration Required)

Who Should Attend:

Aspiring writers, avid readers of psychological thrillers and mystery novels, and anyone interested in the creative journey of a best-selling author.

About the Author:

Jane Doe is a critically acclaimed author known for her gripping psychological thrillers that delve deep into human psyche and societal mysteries. Her works have topped bestseller lists worldwide, captivating readers with their intricate plots and compelling characters. "Whispers in the Stacks" is her fifth novel, exploring themes of hidden truths and the secrets libraries hold.
