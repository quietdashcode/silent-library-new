Kids' Creative Story Time

An enchanting afternoon of stories, songs, and creative crafts designed to spark imagination in our youngest readers!

Join our lively storytellers for a captivating session featuring tales of adventure, friendship, and magic. After the stories, children will participate in a guided craft activity related to the day's theme, fostering their creativity and fine motor skills. This is a perfect opportunity for children to engage with literature in a fun and interactive environment, and for parents to connect with other families.

Event Details:

  • Date: **Monday, August 5, 2025**
  • Time: **2:00 PM - 3:00 PM** (Singapore Time)
  • Location: **Children's Corner, Silent Library**
    123 Library Lane, Knowledge Hub, Singapore 123456
  • Ages: **Recommended for ages 3-7**
  • Admission: **Free** (Registration Required)

What to Bring:

Just bring your imagination! All craft materials will be provided. Please note that parental supervision is required for children under 5.

Why Your Child Will Love It:

  • Engaging storytelling that brings characters to life.
  • Hands-on craft activities that encourage creativity.
  • A friendly environment to make new friends.
  • Develops early literacy skills and a love for reading.
