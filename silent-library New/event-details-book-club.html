Monthly Book Club Meeting

Join fellow book lovers for a stimulating discussion on this month's selection, **"The Midnight Library" by Matt Haig.**

"The Midnight Library" by Matt Haig is a captivating novel that explores themes of choice, regret, and the multitude of lives we could lead. Nora Seed finds herself in a library between life and death, with the opportunity to try out all the lives she might have lived. This thought-provoking book offers a unique perspective on finding happiness and meaning.

Meeting Details:

  • Date: Thursday, August 15, 2025
  • Time: 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM (Singapore Time)
  • Location: Meeting Room 3, Silent Library
    123 Library Lane, Knowledge Hub, Singapore 123456
  • Group: Adults (18+)
  • Admission: Free (No registration required, just show up!)

Discussion Points:

  • What choices would you change if given the chance?
  • How do societal expectations influence our life paths?
  • The role of "regret" in shaping our identity.
  • Favorite alternative lives Nora explored and why.
  • What did you think of the ending?

Join Us:

New members are always welcome! Whether you've finished the book or just started, come and share your thoughts in a friendly and open environment. Light refreshments will be provided, and it's a fantastic way to connect with fellow readers.
