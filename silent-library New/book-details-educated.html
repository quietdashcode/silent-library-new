Educated

By Tara Westover

Availability: Available Now

Summary:

Born to survivalists in the mountains of Idaho, Tara Westover was seventeen the first time she set foot in a classroom. Unable to attend school, she taught herself enough to gain admittance to Brigham Young University. Her journey transformed her, taking her to Harvard and Cambridge Universities. This memoir is a powerful story of self-invention, family loyalty, and the transformative power of education.

Genre: Memoir, Biography, Non-Fiction
