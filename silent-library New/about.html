About Silent Library

Your sanctuary for discovery, knowledge, and endless stories.

Our Mission

At Silent Library, our core mission is to ignite and nurture a lifelong passion for reading and continuous learning. We are dedicated to providing accessible, diverse, and enriching resources that empower individuals to explore new ideas, develop critical thinking, and grow both personally and intellectually. We strive to be a cornerstone of knowledge and culture within the Singapore community.

  • Foster Literacy: Promoting reading habits across all age groups.
  • Ensure Accessibility: Providing a wide range of resources for everyone.
  • Support Personal Growth: Facilitating continuous education and skill development.

Our History

Silent Library was founded in 1985 with a vision to create a peaceful haven for learning amidst the bustling city of Singapore. Starting as a small community initiative, we have grown steadily over the decades, adapting to the evolving needs of our patrons and embracing technological advancements to enhance the library experience. Our journey is a testament to the enduring power of books and the unwavering support of our community.

  • 1985: Established as a community reading room.
  • 2005: Launched our first digital catalog system.
  • 2015: Expanded to include extensive multimedia resources and event spaces.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to meet the diverse needs of our community. From traditional lending to modern digital access, our goal is to provide a seamless and enriching experience for every visitor. Explore our offerings and discover how Silent Library can support your journey of discovery.

  • Vast Collections: Books, e-books, audiobooks, magazines, and research databases.
  • Educational Programs: Workshops, seminars, and literacy initiatives for all ages.
  • Community Events: Author talks, book clubs, and cultural celebrations.
  • Digital Resources: Free Wi-Fi, computer access, and online learning platforms.
  • Quiet Study Zones: Dedicated spaces for focused work and contemplation.
