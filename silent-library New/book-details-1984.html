Nineteen Eighty-Four

By George Orwell

Availability: Available Now

Summary:

A dystopian social science fiction novel and cautionary tale, "Nineteen Eighty-Four" is set in a world of perpetual war, omnipresent government surveillance, and public manipulation, dictated by the Ministry of Truth. Winston Smith, a diligent but disgruntled Outer Party member, secretly rebels against the Party and dreams of freedom, even as he knows he is under constant watch by "Big Brother".

Genre: Dystopian, Political Fiction, Classic
