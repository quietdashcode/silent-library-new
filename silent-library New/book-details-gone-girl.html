Gone Girl

By Gillian Flynn

Availability: Available Now

Summary:

On the morning of his fifth wedding anniversary, Nick Dunne's wife, Amy, disappears. Under pressure from the police and the media, Nick's portrait of a blissful marriage begins to crumble. Soon his lies, deceits, and strange behavior have everyone asking the same dark question: Did Nick Dunne kill his wife? This psychological thriller is known for its intricate plot twists and exploration of marital dysfunction.

Genre: Thriller, Mystery, Psychological Thriller
