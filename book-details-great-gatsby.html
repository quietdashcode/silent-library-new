The Great Gatsby

Summary:

Set in the Roaring Twenties on Long Island, "The Great Gatsby" is a classic American novel. It chronicles Jay Gatsby, a mysterious millionaire, and his obsession with the beautiful Daisy Buchanan. Through the eyes of narrator Nick Carraway, the novel explores themes of idealism, social upheaval, and the American Dream in an era of unprecedented prosperity and moral decay.

Genre: Classic, Literary Fiction, Tragedy
