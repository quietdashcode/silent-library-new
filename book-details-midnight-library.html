The Midnight Library

By Matt Haig

Summary:

Nora Seed finds herself faced with a final opportunity to change her life for the better. When she finds herself in the Midnight Library, she has a chance to undo her regrets and try out new paths. With the help of an old friend, Nora must decide what is truly fulfilling in life, and what makes a life worth living at all.
